About Ratnakar India Ltd

Manufacturer & Exporter of Agro Chemicals

Company Profile

Holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd. is a modern agrochemical company that dedicates to the research, development, production, formulations, export, import, sales & marketing of agro chemical products like pesticides, plant growth regulators, micronutrients, bioagents and fertilers.

Since the beginning, holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd has been thriving to introduce the latest technologies to agricultural industries and farmers to achieve mutual development and prosperity. holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd. devotes to crop protection with the faith of becoming one of the leading ISO standard agrochemical companies in India.

Holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd strictly controls the qualities of products to meet the standard. Under gidence of well qualified persons and standard and Good Manufacture Practice System, final products of holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd are required to undergo strict quality checks corresponding to FAO & CIB specification.

From crop protection products and fertilizers, holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd. has a wide range of selections for agri-product packages, including Insecticide, Pesticide, Fungicide, Herbicide, Plant Growth Regulator and Fertilizers. Supported by the strict quality control system and decades of rich experience, holistic sustainable (India) pvt Ltd is ready for export more than wide range of products with a series of formulations worldwide.

History

We are into the business since 2011. At that we had a presence in Gujarat but currently we are dealing in pan india as well as international market.

When we started we had only 5 products but currently are having more than 150 products across category such as pesticide, insecticide, herbiside, plant growth regulators & technical.

Earlier we just had 5 man power in a single unit plant but currently we have a 3 manufacturing plant and a corporate office in ahmedabad location with a team of 80 + proffesionals.

Current Scenario

We are coming up with a new technical plant, Dahej. With 200 MT per month manujfacturing of MPBD and 200 MT of CMSC.

Our plant is equipped with latest technology in manufacturing of technicals

Our Vision

Production and availability of best quality agrochemicals and other agro inputs to the farming community of the world through new innovation, world class research, best services, inter personal relationship at a very competitive price to maximize the farm productivity with zero environmental pollution.

Our Mission

At Ratnakar India Ltd., our mission is to empower farmers and agricultural professionals globally by providing innovative, high-quality agrochemical solutions. Through continuous research and development, we aim to address the evolving needs of our customers, fostering growth and prosperity in the agricultural sector. We believe in integrity, excellence, and a collaborative approach to make a positive impact on the communities we serve.
